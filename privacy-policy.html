Hyde Privacy Policy

Simple truth: We don't collect any personal data. Your privacy is unbreakable.

1. We Don't Collect Any Personal Data

This app does not collect, store, process, or share any personal information.

We don't track you, we don't analyze you, and we don't send anything to our servers — because we don't have any.

2. Your Files Stay on Your Device

Any photos, videos, or files you use inside the app stay 100% on your device.

We do not access or upload your content anywhere.

3. No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking

We do not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party data processors

Nothing is shared with anyone.

4. Purchases & Subscriptions

Payments are handled completely by Apple through your Apple ID.

We do not receive any personal info from Apple about you.

5. Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever add features that require data collection, we will update this policy and notify you inside the app.
